Few Words About Us

ScienceTech Ltd was formed as a start-up Data Consultancy in 2019 by the team of highly motivated data experts, who can draw on over 70 years of combined experience in data management, data architecture and data solutions.
We are UK-based company with a global outlook, who keeps delivering powerful, practical and innovative data solutions to our customers all over the world.

We help our customers to focus on their biggest business asset – their Data. On a daily basis, we advise companies on how to actively manage data, discover insights from the data and improve quality to make better decisions.

Being a small consultancy company we are proud of our key values and the difference they make.

Our aim is not to sell products or services. Our mission and our priority is to deliver what is right for our customers. We treat all our customers as the long term partners and we invest our knowledge and expertise in their success. Services

Our end-to-end data services are always tailored to deliver your organisation's business objectives

Data Strategy

The ability to meet medium to long-term business objectives requires many aspects of your organisation to work in harmony – all pulling in the same direction. A data strategy underpins your business strategy and sets the agenda for the IT delivery roadmap

Data Architecture

Data is at the centre of everything. That’s why it’s essential to have the right Data Architecture to connect, transport, store, transform and present your data to those who consume it. Your Data Architecture will connect all relevant components with your customers and end users, providing secure access to the insight and value required.

Data Governance

Data Governance drives trust in your data. Data errors can lead to miscommunications, erroneous decisions and erosion of consumer confidence. Data Governance underpins your organisation’s ability to manage and use data assets effectively, and make accurate decisions to drive business performance.

Data Quality Management

Effective Data Quality Management helps you make data-driven decisions that you can trust. We will help you to improve and monitor the health and value of your data, giving you the confidence to drive operations, compliance and analytics initiatives

Data Engineering

Our Data Engineers will ensure your data is well-organised and trustworthy before it is used for BI, reporting, analytics or any other business purpose. Data Engineering is a highly skilled and vital role. After all, the people analysing your data are only as good as the quality of data they’re provided with

Data Compliance and Protection

Data is no longer restricted within four walls of an organization. Systems and applications are becoming increasingly open and interconnected. With data growing at an explosive rate, modern businesses need ways to lower storage and operational costs and at the same time stay compliant to data protection regulations like GDPR to avoid hefty fines
